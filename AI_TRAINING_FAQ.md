   - Investment Range: $50,000 - $249,999

   - Description: Elite tier with maximum returns and priority support

4. **Platinum Tier**
   - Investment Range: $250,000 - $999,999

   - Description: Reserved for future tier expansion

5. **Diamond Tier**
   - Investment Range: $1,000,000+
   - Daily ROI: 0.70%
   - Description: Reserved for future tier expansion

### How are returns calculated?
Returns are calculated daily based on your investment tier. ROI (Return on Investment) is computed automatically every 24 hours from the exact time of your investment. For example, if you invest at 3:15 PM, your ROI accrues at 3:15 PM each day.

### When do I receive my returns?
- **Daily ROI**: Accrues automatically every 24 hours
- **Principal + Returns**: Returned after 30 days (investment maturity)
- **Early Exit**: Available with 5% penalty on principal (accrued yield always returned)

### Are returns guaranteed?
No. All investment returns are indicative and depend on market conditions. Past performance is not indicative of future results. The value of investments can go down as well as up. You may receive less than you invest.

### What happens after 30 days?
After 30 days, your investment matures:
1. Investment status changes to "completed"
2. Principal + accumulated ROI is automatically credited to your liquidity balance
3. A "ROI Payout" transaction is created
4. You receive a notification
5. Funds are available for withdrawal or reinvestment

### Can I invest in multiple plans?
Yes! You can create multiple active investments across different sectors simultaneously. Each investment is tracked separately with its own ROI calculation.

### What is "Liquidity"?
Liquidity is your available cash balance - funds that are ready to invest or withdraw. It includes:
- Deposits that have been confirmed
- Completed investment payouts
- Withdrawal reversals (if rejected)

### What is "Active Principal"?
Active Principal is the total amount currently invested across all your active investments. This money is locked until investment maturity or early exit.

### What is "Total Wealth"?
Total Wealth = Liquidity + Active Principal + Accrued ROI
It represents your complete portfolio value.

### How do I view my investment details?
